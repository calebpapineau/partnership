Planning Hub

Seattle Sounders FC & Seattle Reign FC — integrated commercial, purpose and community planning.

How to use this board

  1. Select **Sounders** or **Reign** below to open the relevant planning board.
  2. Use the preset buttons (Baseline, Growth, Transformational) to load recommended 2026 targets and example metrics.
  3. Customize topline goals (revenue, # of partners, strategic themes) and adjust the per-category metrics in the table.
  4. When you’re ready, click **Export Scenario to Word** to download a `.doc` file formatted as an official club scenario memo. The file name includes the club, season, and scenario code.
  5. Use separate Word exports for ExCo review, partner-specific deal design, and board reporting.
Tip: Treat each scenario as a “portfolio view” of all partnership types in our taxonomy — cash, contra, brand & content, community, digital, and B2B.
