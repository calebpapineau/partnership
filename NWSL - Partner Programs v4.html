NWSL Partnership Matrix

Teams are listed on the left (logos + conference), with partnership type and subtype on the top. Each cell provides the key partner, role, and metric placeholders (value to club, value to partner / KPIs). This is a club-by-club view of key commercial, brand, activation and civic partnerships across the National Women’s Soccer League, with Seattle Reign FC highlighted first. Use the tabs to switch between high-level partnership categories and sub-tabs to drill into specific strategy types (front-of-kit, community impact, etc.).
Data sources: club press releases, NWSL & partner announcements
Monetary estimates: where public/credible; others marked “undisclosed”
Layout: clubs on left, partnership types & subtypes at the top
Note: Values are approximate where cited (e.g., record jersey deals or social media value of kit sponsors) and primarily reflect recent seasons in the last decade. Many NWSL contracts are undisclosed; for those, qualitative benefits are emphasized.
